About Me

Hi, I'm Niraj Patil — a self-taught 3D Artist with a passion for building immersive environments and detailed 3D models. I started my journey with Blender two years ago, learning everything from scratch through the internet — and I haven't stopped since.

I specialize in environment art and architectural modeling — from detailed buildings to expansive landscapes. There's something deeply satisfying about bringing a space to life through geometry, lighting, and materials.

Currently available for freelance projects and always open to exciting collaborations worldwide.
